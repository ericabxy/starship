Beam Weapons, or "What is a Beam Battery Anyway?"
-------------------------------------------------

The [main battery](MainBattery) in these documents represent whatever ship-to-ship weaponry is extant in the fiction the ship lives in. It could be a directed-energy or particle weapon like a _laser beam_, _phaser beam_, or _particle beam_; or something more mundane like _high-velocity projectiles_ or other _ballistic missiles_.
